Welcome to my personal website. It is organized as a digital garden.

What is a digital garden?

Unlike traditional blogging, where articles are published once and for all, in digital gardening, there are no fully finished articles but rather work-in-progress notes. These notes are revised regularly. Like a garden, the notes keep growing over time!

Here is visual representation of my notes and their connections. Each node is a note, and each edge represents a reference. I use org roam to build this. This website is published out of a subset of the entire garden.
