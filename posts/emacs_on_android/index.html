emacs on android

Created: Aug 29 2024, Modified: Aug 29 2024

I have always wanted access to emacs, and especially Org Mode on my android phone.

This note describes my high level setup:

  • orgzly for todo / agenda
  • termux for notes

Orgzly does the job well, but doesn't work well with org roam. On the other hand, termux is good for accessing notes. In termux, I have widgets like open agenda, org capture, search org-roam notes, sync with git.

All my termux configuration is published at termux setup.
